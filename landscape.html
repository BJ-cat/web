Hami City is an ancient city with a long history, and its unique geographical location has created a lot of beautiful scenery. For example, the tranquil Balikun Lake, the stretching Tianshan Mountains, the Mingsha Mountain in the grasslands, and the extensive Yadan landform (commonly known as Devil's City). In addition, there are many historical relics here, such as the tomb of the Hui King a thousand years ago and the beacon towers of various dynasties.
